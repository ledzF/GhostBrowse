# Privacy Policy for GhostBrowse

**Last Updated:** April 27, 2026

## Overview
GhostBrowse is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our extension handles data.

## Data Collection
GhostBrowse **does not collect**:
- Personal information
- Browsing history
- IP addresses
- Identifiable user data

## Data Usage
The extension temporarily sends website URLs to security APIs for analysis:
- **VirusTotal API** - To check for malware and security threats
- **Wayback Machine API** - To check domain age and history
- **Google Safe Browsing API** - To check for known threats

## Data Storage
- Scan results are stored **locally** on your device using Chrome/Firefox storage API
- History is limited to your last 50 scans
- No data is transmitted to external servers except the security APIs listed above

## Third-Party Services
The extension uses:
- VirusTotal API - [VirusTotal Privacy Policy](https://support.virustotal.com/hc/en-us/articles/115002168425-Privacy-Policy)
- Google Safe Browsing - [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- Internet Archive - [Archive.org Privacy Policy](https://archive.org/about/terms.php)

## Your Rights
You can:
- Clear your scan history at any time
- Disable auto-scan in settings
- Uninstall the extension completely

## Contact
For privacy concerns, contact: privacy@ghostbrowse.com

We will update this policy as needed. Check this page for changes.
